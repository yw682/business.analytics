business.analytics

In this assignment, I have run an LDA topic model analysis on Amazon fine food review. The dataset is available at: https://github.com/yw682/business.analytics/blob/master/Reviews.csv. This dataset is found on Kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/snap/amazon-fine-food-reviews).

The topic model result of the top 10 relevant topic is shown as below: The full result is available at: http://127.0.0.1:4321/#topic=0&lambda=0.5&term=

Analysis: We can see that Topic 1,2,3,7, and 10 are closely related. Especially, topic 10 is completely inside of Topic 1. The words in Topic 1 are pretty general, which includes Amazon, fresh, store, buying, and etc. At the same time, other topics have more specific description and nouns. Topic 10 is more about bakery, Topic 2 and 3 are more about ingredients and making dishes. Topic 4,6,8 are more related, while topic 5 and topic 9 are isolated. Base on the frequency of words showing up, and the grouping of the topics, we can see that products relating to bakery, cooking ingredients, and coffee are more popular than others. Some people also like to review the product with the usage of the product, such as how to cook with it.
